While it is still unknown to many people around the world, certain types of people still do not receive education to the same caliber as others. Unfortunately, certain countries boast high literacy rates for males while not so much for females. In fact, in Africa, several countries enroll only 77 women for every 100 men.
Niger: Parity gap of 0.86.
Chad: Parity gap of 0.77
Mali: Parity gap of 89 percent.
Overall, there are still less women being enrolled in education than men in several cases.
